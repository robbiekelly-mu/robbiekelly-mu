How It Works

  • Eraser Tip
    A unique sensor lets you flip Pencil to erase when connected to Paper, without switching tools
  • Bluetooth Smart
    Pencil’s certified Bluetooth Smart wireless delivers a fast, stable connection with industry-leading power conservation.
  • Seamless Body
    Each Pencil is milled from a single, solid piece of material. Graphite brushed aluminum model shown.
  • Lasting power
    Pencil’s battery charges off any USB port in under 90 minutes and delivers power for up to a month of normal use on a single charge.
  • Accurate sensing
    Pencil’s tip and eraser sensors are plated in 14kt gold for lightning fast, accurate reaction.
  • Tapered tip
    Pencil’s durable tip has been carefully friction-tuned to feel great on a screen. Use both the tip and edge to draw. Extra tip and eraser included.
