What is The Fellas?

The Fellas is a UK-based comedy podcast hosted by English Youtubers Calfreezy and TheBurntChip. This podcast is about two fellas keeping you entertained as they go through life trying to figure it all out. The podcast features a wide variety of topics, including stories, football, and Love Island. Many episode include guest features. Episodes air every Tuesday.
